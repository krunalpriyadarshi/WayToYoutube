One of the things that sets Riot Games apart from other game developers is its commitment to fostering a positive community for its players. The company has implemented a number of initiatives to combat toxic behavior and promote sportsmanship in its games. Overall, Riot Games has had a significant impact on the gaming industry, both through its popular games and its efforts to promote positive player behavior and community engagement.
