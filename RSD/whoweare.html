Who We Are

We’re a player-focused, developer-driven company with a mission to deliver great games to the world. We’re the largest game publisher in the Western world, and have committed to building a billion dollar franchise with VALORANT, our premier competitive FPS game.

Founded in 2006, we work hard to build a world where everyone can be their best, authentic selves. We support diversity and inclusivity through every aspect of our company and culture, and work tirelessly to create a supportive and empowering environment for all Rioters.

Our Values

  • Player Experience First: Everything we do starts with putting players first.
  • Challenge Convention: We challenge the status quo, and seek to innovate and push boundaries.
  • Focus on Talent and Team: We value teamwork, collaboration, and personal growth, and seek to attract and retain the best talent.
  • Take Ownership: We take responsibility for our actions and decisions, and own our successes and failures.
  • Stay Hungry, Stay Humble: We approach our work with passion and drive, and never lose sight of our responsibility to our players and our community.

Our Culture

We believe in creating an environment that allows Rioters to do their best work, and that fosters collaboration, creativity, and innovation. Our culture is built on:

  • Diversity and Inclusivity: We value and embrace diversity in all its forms, and work hard to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all Rioters.
  • Empowerment: We trust our Rioters to make smart, informed decisions, and give them the autonomy and support they need to succeed.
  • Continuous Improvement: We believe in always striving to be better, both as individuals and as a company.
  • Fun: We believe in having fun and enjoying our work, and in fostering a positive and supportive community.
