Riot Games Unveils New Character for League of Legends: Gwen, The Hallowed Seamstress

Riot Games recently announced the newest addition to the League of Legends roster, Gwen, The Hallowed Seamstress. A former doll created by a long-lost seamstress, Gwen's lore is steeped in mystery and her unique abilities make her a formidable opponent on the battlefield. Players can now try her out in-game and discover her full potential in the popular MOBA.
