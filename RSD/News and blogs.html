Vampire Survivors

Vampire Survivors: Tides of Foscari is the second DLC expansion for the game that is available on mobile, Xbox, and PC. It will be released in less than two weeks. The DLC adds a new stage, Lake Foscari, where you can explore a mythical forest full of beasts. It also introduces eight new playable characters, such as Eleanor, Maruto, Keitha, and others. You can also use 13 new weapons, like SpellString, Eskizzibur, Prismatic Missile, Flash Arrow, and more. Based on the previous DLC, Vampire Survivors: Tides of Foscari will offer great quality and value.

Princess Connect

Princess Connect Re:Dive (Free), a story-focused game with PvP and PvE modes, will end its service on iOS and Android on April 30, 2023. This is the version by Crunchyroll Games that was released in the West in 2018, not the Japanese version that is still ongoing. The announcement did not give any reason for the shut down, but it thanked the fans of Princess Connect Re:Dive for their support.
